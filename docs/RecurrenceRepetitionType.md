# RecurrenceRepetitionType

The type of the repetition. ndom means: the n-th weekday of the month, where you can also specify which day of the week.

## Enum

* `DAILY` (value: `'daily'`)

* `WEEKLY` (value: `'weekly'`)

* `NDOM` (value: `'ndom'`)

* `MONTHLY` (value: `'monthly'`)

* `YEARLY` (value: `'yearly'`)
